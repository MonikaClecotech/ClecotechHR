Module description

With the increasing demand for optimum and high- quality esthetic restorations, practitioners need the very latest information and practical technique to meet patient needs. The participants will learn to incorporate the latest esthetic materials and techniques into their practice through illustrated lectures, informal discussions, video presentations, and hands-on exercises. This program is specially designed for the practitioner who wishes to focus on incorporating Esthetic dentistry as an integral part of his/her clinical practice. This is the only complete program that encompasses the subject thoroughly and would enable the participant to truly establish himself as an Esthetic Dentist.
